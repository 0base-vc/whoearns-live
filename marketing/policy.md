# WhoEarns X Marketing Guardrails

Account: `@WhoearnsLive`

WhoEarns is an informational Solana validator data account. Posts should explain public validator economics, not promote guaranteed yield or recommend delegation decisions.

## Allowed

- Closed-epoch ranking recaps based on finalized WhoEarns API data.
- Live-epoch previews only when clearly labeled `provisional`, `so far`, and `as of slot/time`.
- Educational posts explaining block fees, Jito tips, skip rate, and methodology.
- Links to WhoEarns dashboards, OpenAPI docs, and GitHub.
- Soft calls to inspect data, such as `Check your validator`.

## Avoid

- `best validator`, `guaranteed yield`, `earn more`, `best APY`, `worst validator`.
- Automatic likes, follows, DMs, quote posts, replies, or unsolicited `@mentions`.
- Tagging validator accounts unless they explicitly opted in.
- Calling live/current epoch rankings final.
- Link-only posts without a data observation.
- Claims that imply investment advice.

## Required Wording

- Closed epoch: `Closed epoch N`
- Current epoch: `Live epoch N preview, provisional as of slot X`
- Ranking basis: `ranked by ...`
- Scope: `validators currently indexed by WhoEarns` unless the dataset truly covers all validators.
- Footer/disclaimer when ranking validators: `Data only, not investment advice.`

## Manual Approval Checklist

- [ ] Source endpoint and timestamp are included in the draft notes.
- [ ] The epoch is closed if the post says `final`, `closed`, or `ranking`.
- [ ] The dataset scope is accurate.
- [ ] No unsolicited `@mentions`.
- [ ] No investment/yield recommendation language.
- [ ] Image text matches the post text.
